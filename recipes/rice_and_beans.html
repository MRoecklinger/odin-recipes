Rice and Beans

Description

Spanish Rice and Beans uses pantry staples to create a nutritious, flavorful one pot meal. Vegetarian, gluten free, and so simple to make, this rice and beans recipe will become a staple.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic gloves
  • some spices
  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • 1 can of fire-roasted diced tomato
  • 2 cans of kidney beans
  • 3 cups of veggie broth

Steps

  1. Put oil in skillet and cook onions until soft
  2. Add all the herbs
  3. Stir in rice until slightly lucid
  4. Add in tomatoes and beans and broth
  5. Let it simmer for 25 minutes
  6. Final sprinkle of lime and some parsley
